Why we built SageReport

School psychologists are expected to be on the frontline of the youth mental health crisis. But most spend the majority of their time writing reports instead of working with students.

We've seen the impact firsthand: burnout, shortages, long waitlists, and kids who need help not getting it fast enough. Research has shown for decades that the biggest barrier to psychologists' impact is the assessment workload, not lack of skill or motivation.

Most enter this field to support children, but their days are consumed by documentation. A single evaluation report can take 6-12 hours to write. That's time taken away from direct student service, from crisis intervention, from the work that actually matters.

We built SageReport to change that. By automating the technical complexity of report writing while keeping you in control of every clinical decision, we're giving school psychologists their time back. Time to spend with students, to support teachers, to be present instead of pressured.

Our Vision

Human connection is the antidote to the youth mental health crisis. SageReport cuts report writing from hours to minutes, freeing school psychologists to spend three times more time with students.

With that time returned, kids get help when they're overwhelmed, teachers get support in hard moments, waits for evaluations shrink, and students reach caring adults faster. This shift brings real relief to schools and puts connection back at the heart of school-based support.
